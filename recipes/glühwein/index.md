## Ingredients
- A bottle of red wine
- One orange
- 2-4 cinnamon sticks
- 10-15 cloves
- 3-4 cardamon pods
- 3-4 star anis
- Sugar

## Steps
- Warm the wine without boiling it.
- Slice orange and add the slices.
- Put the spices in a tea bag and add the bag to the wine.
- Add some sugar and stir.
- Check that it does not boil.
- After around 15 minutes, check the levels of sugar, fruit, and spice.
 - Remove orange or spice bag if necessary.
 - Add more sugar if necessary.
-  Wait another 30 minutes at least.
